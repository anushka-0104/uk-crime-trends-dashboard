Which Areas in England & Wales Are Crime Hotspots?

Key Insights

  • Total offenses recorded: 944,530
  • Highest crime region: London (211,491 offenses)
  • Most common crime type: Violence against the person

What Are the Most Common Types of Crime?

Key Insights

  • Violence Against the Person is the most common crime type in England & Wales.
  • In London, Robbery and Drug offenses are proportionally higher compared to other regions.
